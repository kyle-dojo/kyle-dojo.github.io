Negotiation flows in cycles, not a straight descent.
Upstream (state, stance) shapes downstream (words, outcomes).
Downstream feeds back upstream through response.

Mastery is staying centered inside that loop.

---

## Stance: The Structure You Stand In

You move through four Stances (FEBI Patterns):

* Driver → move things
* Organizer → structure things
* Collaborator → connect things
* Visionary → expand things

Stances are tools, not identities.

So how do you choose?

---

## Meta Compass: Reading the Field

At Mindset Dojo, you orient using the **Meta Compass**:

* What energies are present?
* Are they flowing or stuck?
* Are they trending up or down?

At first, this is mental—**Mind sensing the field**.

But over time, your Meta Compass becomes personal.
Shaped by your values, your experience, your pattern recognition.

From **Gilded Labyrinth → Rooted Orchard**,
you stop navigating complexity externally—
and start sensing clearly from within.

So ask:

**Are you reading the field… or projecting onto it?**

---

## Three Centers: Feeling the Field

Meta Compass begins in the Mind.
**Three Centers completes it in the body.**

* Head → clarity
* Heart → connection
* Gut (Hara) → stability

This is the shift:

* Meta Compass → *interprets* the field
* Three Centers → *feels* the field

When aligned, they converge:

**The Mind stops guessing.
The body already knows.**

---

## Tone: The Signal That Moves Everything

If Stance is structure, **Tone is transmission**.

People don’t respond to your words first—
they respond to your state.

Tone = your nervous system made visible.

You cannot fake it.
You can only **regulate it**.

---

## Hara: The Point of Control

Regulation happens at the **Hara**.

Through practice:

* One Breath → stabilize
* Two Sides → include
* Three Centers → align
* Four Patterns → respond

When the Hara is stable:

* Tone becomes trustworthy
* Stance becomes precise
* The field begins to settle

---

## The Loop

1. Read (Meta Compass)
2. Feel (Three Centers)
3. Stand (Stance)
4. Transmit (Tone)
5. Adjust

Repeat.

---

## Closing Koan

When Meta Compass and Three Centers disagree, which do you trust?

When they align—
who is left to negotiate?
